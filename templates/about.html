About BookWise

What is BookWise?

BookWise is an intelligent book recommendation system that uses collaborative filtering and popularity based to suggest books based on your reading preferences. Simply enter a book you love, and our AI-powered algorithm will find similar books that other readers with similar tastes have enjoyed.

Our system analyzes thousands of book ratings and user preferences to provide you with personalized recommendations that match your reading style.

Technology Stack

  • Flask - Web Framework
  • Pandas - Data Processing
  • NumPy - Numerical Computing
  • Pickle - Model Serialization
  • Bootstrap - UI Framework
  • HTML/CSS - Frontend

How It Works

  1. Enter a book title you enjoy in the recommendation section
  2. Our algorithm finds books with similar rating patterns
  3. Get personalized recommendations based on collaborative filtering
  4. Discover new books that match your reading taste
